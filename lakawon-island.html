LAKAWON ISLAND

Lakawon Island — A Tropical Beach Escape

Lakawon Island is a popular beach destination in Negros Occidental known for its white sand, clear waters, and relaxing atmosphere. Located off the coast of Cadiz, the island offers a perfect getaway for those who want to enjoy the beauty of the sea.

With its peaceful surroundings and scenic views, Lakawon is ideal for both short trips and overnight stays.

White Sand and Clear Waters

The island is famous for its soft white sand and calm, shallow waters. Visitors can swim, walk along the shore, or simply relax while enjoying the ocean breeze.

The clean and well-maintained beach makes it a great spot for families, friends, and beach lovers.

TawHai Floating Bar Experience

One of the main highlights of Lakawon Island is the TawHai Floating Bar, one of the largest floating bars in Asia. Visitors can relax on lounge nets, enjoy drinks, and take in the stunning view of the sea.

It is a unique experience that adds excitement and fun to any visit on the island.

Activities and Relaxation

Aside from swimming, visitors can try kayaking, paddleboarding, and other water activities available on the island. For those who prefer to relax, there are cottages and shaded areas perfect for resting.

The island offers a balance of adventure and relaxation, making it suitable for different types of travelers.

Why Visit Lakawon Island?

Lakawon Island is perfect for anyone looking to enjoy a tropical beach experience in Negros Occidental. It combines natural beauty with fun activities and comfortable facilities.

Whether you want to swim, relax, or enjoy the famous floating bar, Lakawon Island is a must-visit destination.
