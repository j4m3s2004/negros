MASSKARA FESTIVAL

MassKara Festival — The Festival of Smiles

The MassKara Festival is the most famous celebration in Bacolod City, held every October. Known as the “Festival of Smiles,” it is a vibrant event filled with colorful masks, music, and street dancing.

The festival reflects the joyful spirit and resilience of the people of Negros Occidental.

Colorful Street Dancing

One of the main highlights of MassKara is the street dance competition. Performers wear bright costumes and smiling masks as they dance to lively music through the streets.

The performances are full of energy and creativity, making it a must-see experience for visitors.

A Celebration of Culture

MassKara is more than just a festival—it is a celebration of local culture and identity. It showcases the traditions, talents, and creativity of the people of Bacolod.

Various events such as concerts, food fairs, and cultural shows are held throughout the celebration.

Nightlife and Festivities

During the festival, the city comes alive at night with street parties, lights, and live entertainment. Visitors can enjoy music, food, and a festive atmosphere across different parts of the city.

The nightlife during MassKara adds excitement and makes the experience even more memorable.

Why Experience MassKara Festival?

The MassKara Festival is one of the most exciting events in the Philippines. It offers a unique opportunity to experience the happiness, creativity, and culture of Bacolod.

Whether you're watching performances or joining the celebration, MassKara is an unforgettable experience.
