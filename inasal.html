INASAL

Chicken Inasal — A Bacolod Favorite

Chicken Inasal is one of the most famous dishes in Bacolod City. Known for its smoky flavor and unique marinade, it is a must-try for anyone visiting Negros Occidental.

The dish is grilled over charcoal and served with rice, calamansi, and dipping sauces that enhance its flavor.

Unique Flavor and Preparation

What makes Inasal special is its marinade made with local spices, vinegar, and annatto oil, giving it a distinct taste and color.

The grilling process adds a smoky flavor that makes it different from other chicken dishes.

Where to Try Inasal

Visitors can find Chicken Inasal in many restaurants across Bacolod, from small local eateries to well-known chains.

It is widely available and remains one of the top food experiences in the province.

Why Try Chicken Inasal?

Chicken Inasal represents the rich food culture of Bacolod. It is simple, flavorful, and loved by both locals and tourists.

No visit to Negros Occidental is complete without tasting this iconic dish.
